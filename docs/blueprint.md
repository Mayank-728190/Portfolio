# **App Name**: Research Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Landing/Hero Section: Showcase name, title (AI & Data Science Engineer), keywords, and clear CTAs (View Projects, Research & Publications, Contact Me).
- About Me: Describe background, current focus, and future goals (GATE, research roles) with a calm, factual tone.
- Skills Section: Categorize skills into Core CS, AI/ML, Tools & Frameworks, Research & Writing.  Dynamically display skills. Potentially powered by AI tool.
- Projects Section: Detail 4-6 deep projects, highlighting problem, approach, tech used, impact, and status (prototype/research/published).
- Research & Publications: Divide into Published, Under Review, and Completed/Ready, mentioning that acceptance depends on peer-review outcomes.
- Contact Form: A simple contact form will submit messages to the Firestore database.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep indigo (#4B0082) to evoke intellect, focus, and the pursuit of knowledge. The color is associated with research and advanced study. 
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0), a desaturated version of indigo, to provide a clean, neutral backdrop that emphasizes content.
- Accent color: Violet (#8A2BE2), an analogous color to indigo, for highlighting interactive elements and key information to maintain visual consistency while adding a touch of vibrancy.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', sans-serif. Body text: 'Inter', sans-serif.
- Code font: 'Source Code Pro' for displaying code snippets.
- Minimalist design with a focus on clear, structured content sections.
- Subtle transitions between sections to provide a smooth user experience without being distracting.
